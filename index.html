Go Spurs Go!

My favorite NBA franchise is the San Antonio Spurs. They have so many great players. For example, Tim Duncan is the Greatest basketball player ever. David Robinson, is one of the all-time great Centers. George Gervin, is arguably the most underrated player in NBA history. Manu Ginobili and Tony Parker are both great NBA guards who were steals in the draft. Currently, the best Spurs player is Dejounte Murray. He is the youngest player to make an all nba defensive team ever. He is averaging 2 steals per game this season and has the most assists, points, and rebounds of anyone on the Spurs so far in the 2021-2022 season.
